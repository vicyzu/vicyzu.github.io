About the Lab

We are the Vision and Intelligent Computing (VIC) Lab, a research group within the Department of Electrical Engineering at Yuan Ze University. Our team is dedicated to advancing cutting-edge research in Computer Vision, Multimedia Processing, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Intelligent Computing. We specialize in integrating image, video, and audio processing with innovative deep learning techniques to build intelligent and adaptive systems.

Our mission is to design efficient, robust, and scalable AI solutions that address real-world problems across diverse fields, including healthcare, geoscience, human-computer interaction, and autonomous systems. Through strong collaborations with academic partners, industry stakeholders, and international research communities, we aim to push the boundaries of intelligent computing and drive positive global impact.
